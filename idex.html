The "Why" of this Meeting

1. The Mandate

Over the past month, the objective was to conduct a deep-dive diagnosis of your technology ecosystem and operational processes.

2. The Key Finding

The analysis reveals that the core issues are not isolated tool failures, but a lack of standardization, integration, and adoption.

3. The Proposal

This presentation outlines a strategic roadmap designed to solve these root problems and transform technology into a growth engine for the firm.

Strategic Diagnosis: The 3 Key Opportunity Areas

Failure in Adoption & Configuration

Key tools like Karbon are underutilized, forcing teams into parallel systems and causing a loss of traceability.

Fragmented Data Integrity

A 'Unique Client Code' does not exist. Information is not unified, making a 360° view impossible.

Reliance on Manual Processes

Critical tasks like reconciliation, KPI calculation, and Payroll processes consume hundreds of hours.
